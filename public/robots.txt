# JetMeAway — robots directives
#
# Public travel comparison + Scout Reports site (jetmeaway.co.uk).
# Two intents are clearly separated below:
#   1. SEARCH bots — real-time retrieval. Welcomed warmly. They cite,
#      drive traffic, surface us in AI answers.
#   2. TRAINING bots — model-training crawlers. Allowed because
#      brand recall in foundation models = long-tail recommendations.
#      Blocked from /api/, /admin/, /checkout/* same as humans.
#
# All bots, regardless of class, respect the same private-area
# disallows. Those paths leak no public-facing content anyway.
#
# Last updated: 2026-05-08
