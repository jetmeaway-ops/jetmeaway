## 💸 Why the Same Room Costs Different Prices

Large online travel agencies dominate hotel search, and because they're so familiar, many travellers assume they're always cheapest. Often they aren't.

- **Commission pressure:** big online-travel platforms charge hotels a significant commission on every room booked through them. To absorb it, hotels frequently set higher base rates on those channels — and the traveller foots the bill.
- **Hidden wholesale rates:** many suppliers sell rooms in bulk to business-to-business wholesalers, who pass those discounts to consumer sites without giant advertising budgets. Those lower rates exist — they're just hard to find on your own.
- **The illusion of choice:** the cheapest option for your exact dates is often on a site you'd never think to check.
