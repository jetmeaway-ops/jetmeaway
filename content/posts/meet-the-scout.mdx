## The "Old Way" Is Broken

We've all been there. You book a beautiful hotel or an expensive Airbnb, only to show up and realise the nearest gym is a 20-minute Uber away, the "local" coffee shop is a Starbucks, and the neighbourhood feels like a ghost town after 6pm.

Traditional travel sites sell you lodging. They don't sell you a life.

## Enter JetMeAway: The Personal Travel Scout

At JetMeAway, we're not another booking engine. We're an intelligence layer. When you use our Personal Scout, you aren't just finding a bed — you're deploying a digital reconnaissance team to map out your 24-hour ecosystem.

Today we're rolling out the first public build of Scout on Android. iOS follows shortly. This post is the story of why we built it, and what it does that no other travel app does.

## "Deep Neighbourhood" Intelligence

While other apps show you the lobby, the Scout sidebar shows you reality:

- **The morning ritual.** Where is the artisan roastery that actually opens at 6am — not the one that claims to?
- **The fitness ecosystem.** Is there a CrossFit box, a lap pool, or a high-end yoga studio within a five-minute walk of your door?
- **The wellness layer.** Cold plunges, organic grocers, quiet parks, late-night pharmacies, the barber who speaks English — the small things that decide whether a trip feels like home or a hotel.
- **The after-dark map.** Which streets are alive at 10pm and which turn into a car park. The Scout knows.

You tell Scout who you are. It tells you which neighbourhoods fit.

## Privacy Is Our Shield

In an era where your data is auctioned off to the highest bidder, JetMeAway runs differently. Our Privacy Shield means your travel habits, your rituals and your movements stay yours. No resale, no ad-network handoff, no creepy "you searched for Lisbon" emails six months later.

Super-fast information. Zero super-creepy tracking. That's the trade we think travellers should actually be offered.

## The Verdict

The world is too big to stay in the wrong place. Don't just *jet away* — **JetMeAway** to a place that actually fits who you are.

Scout is live on Android today. Download it, set your rituals, and let us map your next city before you land.

**JetMeAway — Life, not just lodging.**
