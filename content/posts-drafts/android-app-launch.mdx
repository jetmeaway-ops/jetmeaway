Today isn't a launch. It's a small takeover of a corner of your phone.

The **JetMeAway: Personal Travel Scout** app is officially available on the Google Play Store. The full Scout engine — compare flights, hotels, car hire, eSIM, packages and insurance from 15+ trusted providers — now fits in your pocket, runs offline when your hotel Wi-Fi gives up, and never asks you to sit through a banner ad.

## Why We Built a Native App

The "big guys" in travel measure success by how long they can keep you inside their funnel. We measure it by how fast we can get you *out of the funnel and into the trip*.

Everything in the Android app is pointed at that single goal.

**Life, Not Just Lodging.** Use the Scout to read the neighbourhood around your hotel, not just the hotel itself. A quiet café two streets from your pool. The gym where the concierge actually trains. The pier where the fishing boats land at 10am. That's what the Scout is for.

**Fast by default.** Native Android, Expo-built, designed for spotty hotel Wi-Fi. No autoplay videos. No upsell carousels. No modal popups begging for your email. Open, search, decide, close.

**Privacy as a first principle.** Your search isn't a product we sell to advertisers. Location data stays on your device unless you explicitly use a feature that needs it (airport detection, neighbourhood mapping). No behavioural tracking, no retargeting cookies, no "personalised" price hikes when you come back to check a fare twice.

**Comparison, not coercion.** Every result shows the provider, the real price, and where you'll be sent to complete the booking. If we earn a commission, you don't pay it — the partner does.

## What's in the First Release

- **Flights** — 250+ UK-origin airports, live search, cheapest/fastest/best sort.
- **Hotels** — 160+ destinations, live pricing, full photo galleries, direct booking via our hotel partners.
- **Packages, car hire, insurance, eSIM** — full parity with the web app.
- **Neighbourhood Scout** — the "deep local" layer UK travel apps usually skip.
- **Offline-first shell** — the app loads and navigates even when the Wi-Fi stalls, and picks up live data the moment it's back.

We held a few things back for v1.1 — group bookings, saved trips that sync across devices, an iOS version — but the core Scout experience is in this release and it's stable.
