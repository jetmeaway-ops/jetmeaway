## Full description (max 4000)

Stop being a tourist. Start being a resident — everywhere.

JetMeAway is a UK travel app that does something no other booking engine does. We don't just sell you a bed. We map the 24-hour ecosystem around your stay — the 6am coffee roastery, the CrossFit box four minutes away, the organic grocer, the quiet park, the street that's alive after dark.

We call it Scout.

### What you can do with JetMeAway

— Search flights across the entire UK market with live pricing
— Compare hotels from multiple trusted suppliers, one clean result per property
— Explore holiday packages and car hire for every major destination
— See the Scout Report for any hotel — the real neighbourhood around it, not just the lobby

### Who JetMeAway is for

If you have ever booked a "perfect" hotel and realised the nearest gym was a 20-minute Uber away, or the only coffee near you was a Starbucks, or the neighbourhood died after 6pm — this app is built for you.

Travellers with rituals. Remote workers. Fitness-minded explorers. Families who want real streets, not sanitised resorts. Students heading abroad for a gap summer.

### Privacy Shield

Your data stays yours. We do not resell your travel habits, your rituals or your location history to anyone. No ad-network handoff, no creepy retargeting. You get super-fast information without the super-creepy tracking.
