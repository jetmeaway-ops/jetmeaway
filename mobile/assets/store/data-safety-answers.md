# Play Store Data Safety Form — JetMeAway Answers

Copy these answers directly into Play Console → App content → Data safety.

## Data collection and security

Does your app collect or share any of the required user data types?
→ **Yes**

Is all user data collected by your app encrypted in transit?
→ **Yes** (HTTPS/TLS on every API call)

Do you provide a way for users to request that their data is deleted?
→ **Yes** (via contact@jetmeaway.co.uk — link in-app and on /privacy)

## Data types collected

### Personal info

Name — **Collected**, **Not shared**. Purpose: Account management, Fulfilment of booking.
Email address — **Collected**, **Not shared**. Purpose: Account management, Fulfilment of booking, App functionality (booking confirmation).
Phone number — **Collected** (optional), **Not shared**. Purpose: Fulfilment of booking (supplier contact).
Address — **Collected** (billing only), **Not shared**. Purpose: Fulfilment of booking.

### Financial info

Payment info — **Collected**, **Not shared**. Purpose: Fulfilment of booking. Note: processed by Stripe, card numbers never touch our servers.
Purchase history — **Collected**, **Not shared**. Purpose: Account management, App functionality.

### Location

Approximate location — **Collected** (optional), **Not shared**. Purpose: Nearest-airport detection, Scout neighbourhood suggestions. User must grant permission.
Precise location — **Not collected**.

### App activity

App interactions — **Collected**, **Not shared**. Purpose: Analytics (Vercel Analytics, aggregated).
Search history — **Collected**, **Not shared**. Purpose: App functionality (Scout remembers recent searches to pre-warm results).

### Device or other IDs

Device or other IDs — **Collected**, **Not shared**. Purpose: Analytics, Fraud prevention (Stripe Radar).

### All other categories

→ **Not collected**. This includes: health & fitness, messages, photos, audio, files & docs, calendar, contacts, web browsing, installed apps, SMS info, passwords.
